3. Job Locking

Prevent:

same node + same job running simultaneously

Critical for stability.

Without this:
same node/job may overlap.

Example:
NTP job takes 40 sec
scheduler interval = 30 sec

Now you get:

duplicate SSH sessions
inconsistent metrics
overload

Enterprise systems ALWAYS implement locking.

==============================

4. Retry Logic

Especially for SSH instability.

Especially:

SSH
transient packet loss
CUCM CLI hangs

BUT:

use controlled retries only

Example:

max retries = 2
backoff = exponential

Otherwise you DDOS your own devices.
